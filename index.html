Who am I?

Greetings, I'm Alex Cameron, an energetic and dedicated recent graduate with a degree in Software Engineering from Carleton University. Ottawa, my hometown, has been the backdrop to my upbringing, supported by my parents and a sister who have played pivotal roles in shaping my values. My professional journey has been enriched by a substantial 16-month Co-op tenure with Gastops Ltd, where I actively contributed to the Software Engineering team.

As I embark on my career, I am driven by a commitment to excellence and a continuous pursuit of knowledge in the ever-evolving field of software engineering.
